FAQs

What are the eligibility criteria to appear for NIMCET-2021 ? +

The eligibility criteria to appear for NIMCET-2021 test are given in Section 2 of the Information Brochure of NIMCET-2021.

Whether Aadhaar card is essential for filling the form? ? +

No, any Government documents (Voter ID card, PAN card, Indian Passport, Driving License issued in India) can be used for identification purposes based on the options available on the website.

What are the criteria and required documents to claim OP-EWS? +

At the time of online application form filling EWS certificate of the current year (i.e. issued on or after April 1, 2020) which is issued by a competent authority is required to be uploaded (duly self-attested scanned copy). Candidates applying in this category must-read clause number 6.1(5) of the Information Brochure of NIMCET-2021

What are the criteria for allotment of rank in NIMCET-2021? +

A candidate scoring zero or negative marks either in Mathematics or in Total Marks in NIMCET-2021 test will be disqualified. Based on the marks obtained by a qualified candidate in NIMCET-2021 test, a rank will be allotted by adopting the criteria specified in the Section 13 of the Information Brochure of NIMCET-2021.
